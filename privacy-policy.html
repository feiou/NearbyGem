Privacy Policy

Information We Collect

Local Data Storage

The following information is stored locally on your device or in your iCloud account:

  • Food preferences and taste settings
  • Restaurant bookmarks and history
  • App settings and preferences

Analytics Data

We collect anonymous usage data through analytics tools to improve our service. This may include:

  • App performance metrics
  • Feature usage statistics
  • Crash reports

All analytics data is anonymized and cannot be linked to individual users.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide personalized restaurant recommendations
  • To improve app functionality through anonymous analytics
  • To maintain and enhance app performance

Data Storage and Security

  • All personal data is stored locally on your device or in your personal iCloud account
  • We do not maintain servers for user data storage
  • We do not transfer your data internationally

Your Rights and Controls

You can:

  • Delete all your data through the app's settings menu
  • Control data storage location (device or iCloud)
  • Opt out of analytics in device settings

Changes to This Policy

We will notify users of any material changes to this policy through app updates.
